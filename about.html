A Message from Olá

I grew up in a house full of beautiful women

My earliest memories are of watching my mother at her vanity — the ritual of it, the confidence she carried when she was done. She made it look effortless. I was fascinated.

Growing up with three sisters, beauty was never just vanity in our home. It was expression. It was power. It was the way the women I loved most showed up for themselves every single day.

I built Browed by Olá for them — and for every woman who deserves a product that truly understands her. Every formula, every shade, every detail is a love letter to the women who shaped me.

We are vegan, cruelty-free, and built from the ground up with inclusivity at our core. Every shade is crafted to complement the full spectrum of skin tones — because every brow deserves precision.

— Olá
